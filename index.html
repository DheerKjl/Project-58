About

Cricket is my favorite hobby. It is my favorite hobby because it is also my favorite sport. My favorite cricketer is Virat Kohli. He is also the captain of the Indian cricket team. My favorite cricket team is India. Cricket is played with a bat and a ball. You have to score runs by hitting the ball and running and also by hitting the ball out of the field.
